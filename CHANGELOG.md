# Change log for [raaz].

## [0.0.1] - June 20, 2016.

* Basic cryptographic types.
* Hashes: sha1, sha256, sha512, sha224, sha384 and their HMACs
* Ciphers: AES-CBC with key-sizes 128, 192 and 256
* Encoding formats: base16
